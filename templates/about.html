About TokyoTorque

Bridging the gap between Japanese automotive excellence and UK enthusiasts

Our Story

Founded in 2022 by lifelong JDM enthusiasts, TokyoTorque Imports was built on a simple belief: UK enthusiasts deserve access to the highest quality Japanese Domestic Market vehicles without the usual hassle, hidden fees, or uncertainty.

What started as a passion project—importing a single R32 Skyline for a private collector—quickly grew into a full-service import business. Today, we've helped over 150 UK customers import their dream JDM cars, from classic Kei trucks to legendary GT-Rs and everything in between.

We're not just importers; we're enthusiasts, drivers, and collectors. We understand the emotional connection to these machines because we feel it too.
