The Import Process

From Japanese auction to UK driveway — we handle everything

1

Vehicle Sourcing

We search Japanese auctions, dealerships, and private sellers to find the exact model, grade, and specification you're looking for. You get full access to auction sheets and vehicle history reports.

📋 Japanese Auction Access 🔍 Grade 4+ Only 📄 Genuine Mileage Guarantee
2

Inspection & Compliance

Once secured, the vehicle undergoes rigorous inspection in Japan. We verify mechanical condition, chassis integrity, and interior quality before accepting the vehicle.

🔧 150-Point Check 📸 Photo/Video Report ✅ Japanese Export Inspection
3

Shipping & Logistics

We arrange RO-RO or container shipping from Japan to UK ports (Southampton, Bristol, or Liverpool). Full marine insurance and real-time tracking provided throughout transit.

🚢 4-6 Week Transit 📦 RO-RO or Container 📍 Real-time Tracking
4

UK Customs & Registration

We handle all UK import procedures: customs clearance, VAT, NOVA notification, IVA testing (if required), and first UK registration with DVLA. You receive a fresh UK V5C logbook.

📝 NOVA Submitted 🇬🇧 IVA Testing 🪪 UK Registration 🔢 Age-Related Plates
5

Handover & Aftercare

The final step. We prepare your vehicle with UK MOT, fresh service, and valet. You collect your car from our UK facility or we can arrange UK-wide delivery. Full aftercare support included.

🧼 Full Valet 🔧 Pre-Delivery Service 🚛 UK Delivery Available 📞 12-Month Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the whole process take?

Typically 8-12 weeks from auction win to UK delivery. Sourcing the right vehicle can take longer depending on rarity.

Do you import cars older than 10 years?

Yes! Vehicles over 10 years old don't require IVA testing, making the process simpler and cheaper.

What Japanese auction access do you have?

We have direct access to USS, TAA, and CAA auctions — Japan's largest wholesale marketplaces.

Can you import any JDM car?

We specialise in performance JDM vehicles but can import any Japanese domestic market car, including Kei cars, vans, and classic models.

Do I need to visit Japan?

Not at all. We handle everything remotely. You can view auction sheets, photos, and videos from your phone.

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfer (Faster Payments), GBP or JPY. We require a 20% deposit to secure a vehicle, balance on arrival.
